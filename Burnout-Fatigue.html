A holistic approach to long-lasting weight loss.

a Side Benefit - not the Main Goal

At Sukhavati, we understand that many people come to health retreats specifically wanting to lose weight. In many cases
(80-90%) our guests do lose weight. However, it is important to note that this is not the primary focus here.

From the Ayurvedic perspective, the main goal is to help you achieve your optimal state of mental, physical, and emotional balance. When our bodies are in their optimal state of balance, they naturally detox on a daily basis, they are naturally resistant to stress and effortlessly maintain an ideal weight.

According to their constitution and current state of balance or imbalance, some people do need to lose weight to achieve
balance. Others do not. By focusing holistically and using the deeper wisdom of Ayurveda, we restore the doshas to balance. As a natural result, our health at every level improves.

Our focus at Sukhavati is on guiding you toward true balance and optimal health rather than solely focusing on specific outcomes like weight loss or temporary stress relief.

Our timeless approach addresses the root cause of imbalances in the body and mind, allowing you to naturally return to your healthiest, most vibrant state. In this state of balance, unnecessary weight is shed, and stress is naturally dissolved.

Other benefits include:

  • Feeling lighter and more energized

  • Improved digestion and elimination

  • Being the perfect weight for your body type

  • Clearer, more radiant skin

  • Deeper, more rejuvenative sleep

  • Increased happiness and positivity

  • Improved resilience to stress

  • Better relationships, stemming from feeling better within yourself
