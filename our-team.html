ayurvedic eye treatment

Netra Tarpana

Medicated ghee is poured into a dough bridge over the eyes. The eyes are intermittently opened and closed for a short period. Eye dryness caused by aggravation of the Vata or Pitta Dosha is commonly treated by Netra Tarpana.

- Helps prevent cataracts, blurred vision
- Helps improve visual acuity and eyesight
- Helps reduce eye infections, glaucoma/retinopathy, and sore or dry eyes
