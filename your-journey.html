How long should I stay at Sukhavati?

SHORT RETREATS (3-6 days)

For those with limited time, a 3–6 day stay offers a perfect introduction to the rejuvenating benefits of Sukhavati. Focused on relaxation and pampering, these shorter retreats allow you to enjoy our delicious vegetarian cuisine, soothing treatments, and the peaceful natural surroundings, leaving you feeling refreshed and renewed.

MEDIUM RETREATS (1-2 Weeks)

A 7–14 day stay is ideal if you are seeking deeper rest and rejuvenation, and particularly if you are wanting to complete the traditional Ayurvedic Panchakarma process. This extended program includes a deeper level of purification, designed to remove deep-seated toxins, physical and emotional blockages and set the foundation for lasting wellness.

EXTENDED RETREATS (2-4 Weeks)

If you're looking to promote healing or recovery from a chronic illness or have the desire to achieve and maintain the highest possible health you can, a 21-28 day stay can be life-changing. While most guests come for 5, 7 or 14 days (often once a year) and get incredible results, slightly longer stays are ideal where deeper imbalances or burnout needs to be resolved as well giving you plenty of time to fully and gently integrate all the profound transformation.
